This onboarding plan helps integrate new IT team members into the company efficiently.

Day 1: Orientation & Introductions

  • Team introductions and office tour
  • Overview of ISR goals and values
  • Company policies and handbook review
  • Login credentials and ID setup

Day 2: Systems Access & Shadowing

  • Access to ticketing/email systems
  • Overview of software/tools
  • Shadowing senior analyst
  • Intro to KPIs and SLAs

Day 3: First Tasks & Performance Metrics

  • Assigned low-priority service requests
  • Practice using ticket system
  • Review customer communication protocols
  • Walkthrough of performance expectations
